About HourBank

Building equitable communities through time-banking

Our Mission

HourBank creates equitable communities where everyone's time has equal value. Whether you're a lawyer or a gardener, one hour of your service equals one hour of credit in our time-banking system.

How Time Banking Works

1. Offer Services

Share your skills - from tutoring to home repairs, cooking to coding.

2. Earn Hours

Help community members and earn time credits for every hour you contribute.

3. Spend Hours

Use your earned credits to access services you need from other members.

4. Build Community

Create lasting relationships through mutual aid and shared experiences.

Our Values

Equality

All time is valued equally, regardless of skill type or market demand.

Community

Building connections and mutual support within neighborhoods.

Accessibility

Everyone can participate, regardless of economic status or "marketable" skills.

Sustainability

Creating resilient communities that support each other long-term.

Community Impact

Time banking has been proven effective for over 40 years, with 4,000+ time banks operating globally. HourBank brings this powerful concept into the digital age with modern tools for scheduling, messaging, and community building.
