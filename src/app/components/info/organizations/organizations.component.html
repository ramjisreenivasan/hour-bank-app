How Organizations Use HourBank

Corporate Volunteering Programs

Companies sponsor employee time-banking participation, allowing staff to contribute skills during work hours while building stronger local communities.

Non-Profit Volunteer Coordination

Non-profits use HourBank to organize volunteers, track service hours, and create reciprocal relationships where volunteers can also receive community support.

Educational Community Service

Schools and universities integrate time-banking into community service requirements, teaching students about equity and mutual aid while serving local needs.

Local Business Community Investment

Local businesses sponsor community hours, provide meeting spaces, or offer services through the platform to strengthen their neighborhood connections.
