How We Stay Free

Sustainable business model that keeps time-banking accessible to all

Our Commitment

The core time-banking system will always be completely free. We believe everyone deserves access to community support, regardless of their ability to pay.

Freemium Community Model

HourBank operates on a sustainable freemium model where 95% of users enjoy full access to time-banking features at no cost, while premium services and business partnerships generate revenue to support the platform.

Premium Features ($9.99/month)

  • Advanced scheduling and calendar integration
  • Cross-community participation
  • Enhanced analytics and insights
  • Priority customer support

Optional enhancements for power users - never required for basic time-banking.

Business Partnerships

  • Corporate employee volunteering programs
  • Non-profit volunteer coordination tools
  • Educational institution community service tracking
  • Local business community sponsorships

Organizations that benefit from stronger communities support the platform.

Platform Extensions

  • Tool and equipment rental coordination
  • Skill certification and verification programs
  • Insurance and bonding for specialized services
  • White-label licensing for other communities

Value-added services that enhance the community experience.

Financial Transparency

We believe in being open about how we sustain the platform:

  • No transaction fees - We never take a cut of time exchanges
  • No advertising - Your data and attention are not for sale
  • Community-first - Profits are reinvested in platform improvement
  • Open governance - Community input guides our development priorities

Social Impact Investment

HourBank attracts investors who care about both financial returns and social impact. Our model proves that building stronger communities can be both profitable and purposeful.

Community Value

Every hour exchanged creates social connections and reduces inequality

Economic Impact

Time banking provides access to services regardless of economic status

Environmental Benefit

Local service exchange reduces transportation and resource consumption
