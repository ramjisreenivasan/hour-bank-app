What is hOurBank?

hOurBank is a revolutionary time-banking platform that enables people to exchange skills and services using time as currency. One hour of your time equals one hour of someone else's time—regardless of the service provided.

Whether you're a software developer, a gardener, a teacher, or a chef, your time has equal value in our community. This creates a truly democratic economy where everyone can contribute and benefit.
